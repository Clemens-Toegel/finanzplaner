# Privacy Policy for Pilo

**Effective date:** 8 February 2026

Pilo respects your privacy.

## Summary

Pilo is designed to work primarily on your device. Your expense data, notes, and scanned receipts are stored locally on your phone.

## Data We Collect

We do **not** collect personal data on our servers.

Pilo does not require account creation and does not send your expense data to a backend operated by us.

## How Data Is Used

Data in the app is used only to provide core features, including:

- expense tracking
- receipt/document scanning (OCR)
- exporting reports (PDF/Excel)

OCR processing is performed on-device.

## Camera and Photo Access

Pilo may request access to:

- **Camera** (to scan receipts/documents)
- **Photos** (to select receipt images)

These permissions are used only for app functionality you initiate.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

Data is only shared when you explicitly choose to export or share files from your device.

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it (or uninstall the app).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new effective date.
